Featured Projects

AI Mock Interviewer

Most people only realise they're underprepared when they're already in the interview. I built a conversational AI web app that simulates real technical interviews — generating progressive questions, adapting to your answers, and giving structured feedback so you can improve before it counts. Built with Mistral AI, Flask, and deployed live in 8 days.

STRIDE

Wellness apps are either too complex or too shallow. STRIDE combines journaling, habit tracking, and event planning into one clean experience — built around the idea that healthy routines should feel manageable, not overwhelming.

Virtual Zoo

An interactive exhibit platform designed for real users, tested at a university expo. Two versions were built and evaluated against each other using questionnaires and statistical analysis in R — because good design should be measured, not assumed.
